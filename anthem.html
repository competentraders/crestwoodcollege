Crestwood College Anthem

Motto: Ad Astra Per Laborem ("To the Stars Through Hard Work") Verse 1 From hills of hope we proudly rise,
With truth and wisdom as our prize.
In Crestwood's halls our dreams take flight,
Guided by knowledge, shining bright.
Through every challenge, strong we stand,
United heart and hand in hand.
Our dreams take flight, our spirits soar,
To greater heights forevermore.

Chorus

Crestwood College, proud and true,
Our hearts belong to you.
Ad Astra Per Laborem,
Our guiding light each day.
With knowledge, faith, and noble deeds,
We'll lead with courage on our way.
Crestwood College, ever bright,
We'll lift your banner high!

Verse 2

Within your halls our minds are shaped,
By discipline our lives are made.
We learn to serve with humble grace,
To leave the world a better place.
In every field, in every land,
We'll carry forth your noble brand.
We'll build tomorrow hand in hand.

Chorus

Crestwood College, proud and true,
Our hearts belong to you.
Ad Astra Per Laborem,
Our guiding light each day.
With knowledge, faith, and noble deeds,
We'll lead with courage on our way.
Crestwood College, ever bright,

Bridge

Though years may pass and paths may part,
Your noble spirit fills each heart.
Forever loyal we shall be,
A shining Crestwood family.

Final Chorus

Crestwood College, proud and true,
Our hearts belong to you.
Ad Astra Per Laborem,
We'll strive until we shine.
With honor, excellence, and love,
Your legacy is mine.
Crestwood College, evermore,
Our beacon through all time!

Amen.

About Crestwood College

Building Character • Inspiring Excellence • Creating Leaders

Our History

Founded in 1892, Crestwood College has remained committed to providing quality education rooted in integrity, discipline, and excellence. Over the years, the college has produced distinguished graduates who have excelled in medicine, engineering, law, business, public service, technology, education, and entrepreneurship. Today, Crestwood College continues to nurture future leaders through innovative teaching, modern facilities, and strong moral values.

Our Vision

To become a world-class institution recognized for academic excellence, innovation, leadership, and character development.

Our Mission

To educate students through quality teaching, ethical leadership, critical thinking, creativity, and lifelong learning.

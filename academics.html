Academic Programme

Crestwood College offers a balanced curriculum that combines academic excellence, leadership development, creativity, innovation, and moral discipline. Our teaching methods encourage critical thinking, collaboration, communication, and lifelong learning.

Junior Secondary School

Our Junior Secondary programme lays a solid academic foundation while developing confidence, creativity, and good character.

Senior Secondary School

Students prepare for WAEC, NECO, JAMB, Cambridge Checkpoint, and other national and international examinations.

Sixth Form Programme

Advanced preparation for university admission through A-Level and foundation programmes.

Admissions

Your Journey to Excellence Begins Here

Join the Crestwood Family

Thank you for considering Crestwood College. We admit students who demonstrate academic potential, good character, and a willingness to learn. Admissions are open for Junior Secondary, Senior Secondary, and Sixth Form classes.

Step 1

Complete the online application form.

Step 2

Submit supporting documents and passport photograph.

Step 3

Attend the entrance examination and interview.

Step 4

Receive your admission offer and complete registration.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed Application Form
  • Birth Certificate
  • Two Recent Passport Photographs
  • Previous School Result
  • Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • Medical Fitness Report
  • Parent/Guardian Identification
